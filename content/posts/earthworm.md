# Earthworm

<div class="poem">
Caught up in leaves  
like earthworms  
under the rain.  
One without direction…  
on the distant muddy terrain.  

No future in mind…  
No past in sight…  
All that remains  
is its slimy head, over-strained.  

Unlike a caterpillar,  
an earthworm stays the same…  
Under the rain… or the sun… or thunder…  
it has only one name.  

But an earthworm also grows one day…  
not in the hope of flying away…  

But in the hope that it accepts to be  
just another earthworm some day.
</div>
