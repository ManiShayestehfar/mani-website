The task of estimating stellar ages in the Milky Way is of great importance for Galactic Archaeologists today. In this paper, we utilised the GALAH DR4 data to investigate the further possibility of estimating stellar ages in the Milky Way through XGBoost’s gradient-boosting platform as a predictive model.
