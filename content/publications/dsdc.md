The fundamental mysteries of quantum mechanics are closely tied to its departures from classical optics. In three papers we analyse John A. Wheeler's delayed-choice experiment, showing that it admits a classical, non-retrocausal explanation. In the third paper, I independently present an optical analogue of the quantum eraser.
